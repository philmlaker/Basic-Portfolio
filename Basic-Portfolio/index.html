About Me

Born and raised in 3 feet of snow in northern Minnesota. I graduated with a BS degree in biology and chemistry minor in 2010 and subsequently moved to Denver, CO right after. In 2013 I became certified as a Clinical Laboratory Scientist through ASCP and began working in the Molecular and Virology Department at University of Colorado Hospital.

Moved to Austin in 2015 and absolutely loving it. Austin is the city I was meant to live in. I love the weather, the people, and the the city. I am so happy I moved here. I love Torchies Tacos and the Salt Lick.
